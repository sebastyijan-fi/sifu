Welcome to SIFU - A Bachelor Thesis Project

The aim of this project is to explore AI interaction and contribute to the future of AI communication.

If you choose to press on participate, you will be given an assigment and you will need to interact with an AI assistant. When the counter hits 0 you will be automatically redirected(after 8 seconds) to a questionnaire. You can participate as many times as you want, just remember to mark it on the questionnaire.


Note: We collect anonymous data (your AI interactions and questionnaire responses) solely for academic research, without personal identifiers. Your participation implies consent to this data collection.
